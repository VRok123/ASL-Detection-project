About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to make American Sign Language (ASL) more accessible and easier to understand for everyone. We aim to bridge the communication gap between the hearing and deaf communities.

How It Works

This project uses advanced machine learning techniques to recognize ASL gestures and translate them into text or speech. Whether you're learning ASL or just curious, we hope you find this tool useful!
